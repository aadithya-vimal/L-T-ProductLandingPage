Specifications

  • Engine: 998cc, 4-cylinder, supercharged
  • Power: 310+ HP (326 HP with RAM air)
  • Frame: Trellis, high-tensile steel
  • Weight: 216 kg (476 lbs)
  • Top Speed: 400+ km/h (248+ mph)
  • Brakes: Dual semi-floating 330mm discs, Brembo calipers
  • Electronics: KTRC, KLCM, KIBS, KEBC, Quick Shifter

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the H2R street legal?
No, the H2R is a track-only motorcycle and is not legal for street use.
How much does the H2R cost?
The price varies by region, but it is one of the most exclusive and expensive superbikes in the world.
What makes the H2R unique?
Its supercharged engine, carbon fiber body, and advanced electronics set it apart from any other motorcycle.
